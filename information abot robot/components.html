Robot Components

  • Servo Motors: Used for precise control of angular position in the robot's joints or mechanisms.
  • BO Motors: Small and efficient DC motors that drive the robot's wheels.
  • Arduino Uno: The microcontroller used to program and control the robot's operations.
  • Motor Driver: Acts as an interface between the microcontroller and motors, allowing for bidirectional control.
  • Ultrasonic Sensor: Measures distance to objects and helps in detecting obstacles.
  • IR Sensors: Detects human presence by identifying changes in infrared radiation.
