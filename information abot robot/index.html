About the Human Detecting Robot

The human-detecting robot is a sophisticated machine designed to identify and track humans using advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms. It is capable of detecting human presence in crowded areas, buildings, and outdoor spaces.

Technology Behind the Robot

  • Machine Learning: Processes sensor inputs to recognize human shapes and movements.
  • Sensors: Infrared, ultrasonic, and cameras enable real-time detection and tracking.
  • AI Integration: Built-in AI ensures continuous learning and improved accuracy over time.
